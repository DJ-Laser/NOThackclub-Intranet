Intranet

Intranet is a backpacking-based hackathon, similar to trail.

  1. No WiFi
  2. Forget Google; Code like it's 1999
  3. Deep connections and make lifelong friends
  4. Learn to develop software disconnected
  5. A group of hackers who know how to keep it real
  6. Learning the importance of preparation
  7. Have an answer that doesn’t make you sad to ‘what did you do over the summer’
  8. Reconnect with nature

Steps

  1. Join #intranet on the Hack Club Slack
  2. Find some people you know
    (hacking is better with friends)
  3. Attend the kickoff call March 5
  4. Attend the 2 weekend hackathons to get the ropes before the big event
    (optional, but recomended if you haven't gone on a backpacking trip)
  5. Travel to the trail using Hack Club's Gas Fund or a $200 flight stipend if you live ≥ 1500mi/2400km away
  6. Go on a once-in-a-lifetime experience
