Intranet

Intranet is a backpacking-based hackathon, similar to trail.

Steps

  1. Join #intranet on the Hack Club Slack
  2. Find some people you know (hacking is better with friends)
  3. Attend the kickoff call March 5
  4. Attend the 2 weekend hackathons to get the ropes before the big event (optional, but recomended if you haven't gone on a backpacking trip)
